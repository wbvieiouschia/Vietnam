FESTIVAL AND EVENT TOURISM

Vietnam’s festivals and events are colorful, immersive, and rooted in centuries of culture. Tourists can witness traditions come alive through music, dance, food, and ceremonies.

Tet (Lunar New Year)

The most important Vietnamese holiday, celebrating the start of the lunar calendar year.

Family Reunions Bánh Chưng & Festive Foods Street Markets & Dragon Dances
Families gather, clean homes, and honor ancestors to ensure good luck and prosperity. Traditional sticky rice cakes with mung bean and pork; seasonal foods and sweets are prepared and shared. Public celebrations include dragon dances, street decorations, and local festivals that tourists can join.

Regional Festivals

Celebrating local culture and traditions through unique regional events.

Lim Festival (Bac Ninh) Hue Boat Racing Festival
Celebrates Quan Ho folk songs, cultural rituals, and traditional games. Visitors can join performances and competitions. Competitive boat races on the Perfume River, featuring music, ceremonial rituals, and local festivities.

Mid-Autumn Festival

Family-oriented celebration with lanterns, mooncakes, and children's activities.

Lantern Parades Mooncake Tasting Children's Games & Activities
Colorful lantern processions light up streets and rivers; tourists can create lanterns themselves. Sample traditional mooncakes filled with sweet or savory fillings during family gatherings. Games, storytelling, and cultural performances for children; visitors can participate in workshops and parades.

Modern Cultural Events

Contemporary festivals, art shows, and performances reflecting Vietnam’s evolving culture.

Art Exhibitions Music Festivals Fashion Shows International Performances
Galleries showcase local and emerging artists, contemporary visual arts, and interactive exhibits. Outdoor and indoor music festivals with local and international performers. Fashion shows highlight modern design while incorporating traditional textiles and motifs. International cultural performances bring global perspectives to Vietnamese stages.

Participation Opportunities

Tourists can actively engage with festivals through rituals, dance, and culinary experiences.

Rituals & Processions Traditional Dance Lessons Cooking Classes
Participate in ceremonial rites and festive processions with local communities. Learn traditional Vietnamese dance forms guided by festival instructors. Prepare festival dishes under local guidance; gain hands-on culinary experience.
