BUSINESS & CONFERENCE TOURISM

Vietnam is buzzing with business opportunities! Professionals from all over the world come for meetings, conferences, trade fairs, and corporate events in this vibrant country.

Modern hotels, smart convention centers, and a warm, cultural welcome make business travel here both productive and enjoyable.

MICE Tourism

MICE Tourism Growth

Vietnam is becoming a hotspot for Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions (MICE), attracting professionals worldwide.

Major Convention Cities

Cities like Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, and Da Nang host modern convention centers and international hotels perfect for global events.

Advanced Facilities

Conference halls, breakout rooms, high-tech AV setups, and professional services make every event smooth and impressive.

Business Events & Forums

Leadership summits, regional conferences, and international forums are held regularly, drawing participants from around the globe.

Cultural Experiences

Attendees can enjoy cultural performances, taste local cuisine, and explore sightseeing spots alongside their professional schedule.

Corporate Travel

Business travelers may participate in factory tours, startup visits, or tech demos to explore Vietnam’s growing industries.

Business Facilities & Luxury Stay

Business Hotels

High-quality hotels and resorts cater perfectly to business travelers and corporate events.

Professional Workspaces

Executive meeting rooms, coworking spaces, and fast Wi-Fi make work easy anywhere.

Leisure Facilities

Relax after work in spas, rooftop lounges, restaurants, or fitness centers.

Bleisure Travel

Extended Travel Experience

Business travelers often extend their stay to explore Vietnam’s attractions and culture.

Tourism Activities

Visit landmarks, beaches, heritage sites, and local markets during free time.

Work-Life Balance

Culinary tours, entertainment, and guided excursions help travelers unwind after work.

Business and conference tourism helps Vietnam grow economically, attract investors, and build global connections while offering visitors a cultural and professional experience.
